Exploring Chicago's popular bike-share program "Divvy"

IMT 562 - Interactive Data Visualization (UW Winter 2020)

Divvy Bikes

Divvy is Chicago's bike share system, with over 600 stations and 6,000+ bikes across Chicagoland. It’s a fun, affordable and convenient way to get around. We want to explore the most popular routes in terms of rider traffic and come up with recommendations to improve the bike-share program based on the rider usage patterns during the various times of the day.
